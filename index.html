How to Play

Master the art of the snake

🎮

Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to navigate. Press SPACE to pause the game anytime.

🍎

Objective

Guide your snake to eat the glowing red food. Each piece makes you grow longer.

⚡

Speed

The snake moves faster with each food eaten. Stay sharp as difficulty increases!

💀

Game Over

Don't hit the walls or bite yourself. One mistake and it's game over!

Pro Tip 💡

Plan your moves ahead! The snake follows your commands instantly, so think about where you'll be in the next few seconds to avoid trapping yourself.

🐍

About Snake

Snake is a classic video game that originated in the late 1970s. The concept is simple but addictive: control a growing line (the snake) to eat items while avoiding colliding with yourself or the walls.

This modern recreation pays homage to the original while bringing contemporary design aesthetics and smooth gameplay to your browser.
